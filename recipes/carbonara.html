Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is the shit and honestly mine looks better than the one in this picture. It takes like 30 min to make and uses like 5 ingredients, so it's a great staple.

Ingredients

  • Quarter circumference - Spaghetti Noodles
  • 2 Eggs - 1 whole and 1 yolk
  • 1 oz - Grated parmesan
  • 1 oz - Grated romano
  • 2 Strips - Thick cut bacon
  • Generous amount - Crushed black pepper

Steps

  1. Slice the bacon in to small pieces and place in pan (no oil). Set heat to medium/low and cook until done, flipping occasionally.
  2. While bacon is cooking, add water to a pot and start bringing to a boil.
  3. While bacon is cooking and water is reaching boil, add cheeses, egg, and black pepper to small bowl. Whisk with fork until incorporated.
  4. Water should be close to boiling at this point, add noodles once it is and cook to al dente.
  5. Bacon should also be close to done at this point. Once cooked, kill heat and let cool down. Once cooled, drain most of grease from pan.
  6. Once noodles are done, throw into bacon pan and pour sauce on top. Mix together with tongs. Keep it moving so that egg doesn't cook.
  7. Plate and serve!
