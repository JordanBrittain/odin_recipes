Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is the shit and honestly mine looks better than the one in this picture. It takes like 30 min to make and uses like 5 ingredients, so it's a great staple.

Ingredients

  • Quarter circumference - Spaghetti Noodles
  • 2 Eggs - 1 whole and 1 yolk
  • 1 oz - Grated parmesan
  • 1 oz - Grated romano
  • 2 Strips - Thick cut bacon
  • Generous amount - Crushed black pepper
